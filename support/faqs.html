Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a test ride?

You can book a test ride through our website by clicking on the "Book Test Ride" button, or by visiting your nearest Hero MotoCorp dealership. Our team will contact you to schedule the test ride at your convenience.

What is the recommended service interval?

We recommend servicing your vehicle every 3,000 kilometers or 3 months, whichever comes first. Regular maintenance ensures optimal performance and longevity of your vehicle.

What is covered under warranty?

Our standard warranty covers manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship for a period of 2 years or 30,000 kilometers, whichever comes first. Additional warranty terms may apply to specific components.

How can I purchase genuine parts?

Genuine Hero MotoCorp parts can be purchased from any authorized dealership or service center. You can also order parts online through our official website.

Our customer support team is here to help you.
